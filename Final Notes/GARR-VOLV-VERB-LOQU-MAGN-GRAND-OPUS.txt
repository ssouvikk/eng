GARR-          → chatter, prattle

VOLV- / VOLU-  → roll, turn

VERB-          → word
LOQU-          → speak

MAGN-          → great, large
GRAND-         → great, impressive

OPUS           → work

RE-            → back, again
E- / EX-       → out

-ITY           → state, quality
-AGE           → collection, condition
-ATION         → process, result
